cheers

HOW TO PLAY

CHEERS is a fast-paced, real-time, multiplayer game you can play anywhere.

Play when around a bonfire with friends or around the table with family.
It’s especially great when you’re stuck at home but still want to have fun with friends or family!

PRE-GAME

Click the 'JOIN GAME' button to enter the game.

Enter the name you will use for the duration of the game and click 'JOIN' to enter the waiting lobby.

IMPORTANT! Don't click 'LET'S GO' until all your friends' names are visible on your screen.

When all your friends are in the lobby, choose one person to click 'LET'S GO' to start the game.

IN-GAME

During a game of CHEERS you will encounter several types of prompts.


TRUE OR FALSE

These are basic TRUE or FALSE questions. Simply click the button you feel is the correct answer.

SINGLE OPEN-ENDED ANSWER

A single open-ended prompt will ask you to enter in ONE answer. Enter this text in the provided text box and click 'SUBMIT'.
Once your answer is submitted you will be brought to the answer display screen. Other player's answers will appear when all players submit their answers.
When all answers are displayed, click the answer you like the most.

A winner will be selected based off everyone's votes. The winning response and their name is displayed along with an action that person takes.

DOUBLE OPEN-ENDED ANSWER

Similar to the SINGLE OPEN-ENDED ANSWER, it will ask for your text response. However, you will be prompted to enter TWO responses. Enter your two responses in the text box, but separate them with a single or double space.
Winners and their response are selected the same as above.
